# 🧮 Example 1.2 (Decimal → Binary)

👉 given:

```text
84₁₀
```

---

## 🔹 Method 1 (Power method 🔥)

👉 largest power of 2 ≤ 84:

```text
64 ✅
```

---

### Step-by-step

```text
84 - 64 = 20 → 1  
20 < 32 → 0  
20 - 16 = 4 → 1  
4 < 8 → 0  
4 - 4 = 0 → 1  
2 → 0  
1 → 0  
```

---

👉 result:

```text
1010100₂ ✅
```

---

## 🔹 Method 2 (Divide by 2 🔥)

👉 divide repeatedly:

```text
84 ÷ 2 = 42 remainder 0  
42 ÷ 2 = 21 remainder 0  
21 ÷ 2 = 10 remainder 1  
10 ÷ 2 = 5  remainder 0  
5 ÷ 2 = 2   remainder 1  
2 ÷ 2 = 1   remainder 0  
1 ÷ 2 = 0   remainder 1  
```

👉 reverse:

```text
1010100₂ ✅
```
