❌ handmade
❌ slow
❌ expensive

## 🔹 Henry Ford revolution

✔️ mass production

---

# 🧠 Meaning என்ன?

👉 Ford சொல்றது:

👉 parts:

👉 benefit:

✔️ fast assembly
✔️ easy repair

# 🧠 Computer context (VERY IMPORTANT 🔥)

---

## 🔹 Analog circuits

👉 continuous values:

```text id="l3laxd"
0.1V, 0.53V, 2.87V...
```

👉 ❌ complex
👉 ❌ hard to design

---

## 🔹 Digital circuits

👉 only:

```text id="z6z3wo"

```

👉 ✔️ simple
👉 ✔️ predictable

---

# ⚡ Key insight

✔️ easier
✔️ scalable
